Data Science JOB DESCRIPTION
We are seeking a highly motivated Data Scientist to join our analytics team. The ideal candidate will have strong analytical skills and a passion for discovering insights from large datasets.
Responsibilities:
* Collect, clean, and preprocess structured and unstructured data.
* Build and deploy predictive models using machine learning algorithms.
* Analyze trends and patterns to support business decision-making.
* Collaborate with engineering and product teams to implement data solutions.
* Communicate results effectively through dashboards and reports.
Required Skills:
* Proficiency in Python, SQL, and Pandas.
* Experience with machine learning libraries (e.g., scikit-learn, TensorFlow).
* Knowledge of statistics, data wrangling, and feature engineering.
* Familiarity with data visualization tools like Matplotlib, Seaborn, or Power BI.
* Experience with cloud platforms (AWS/GCP/Azure) is a plus.
Qualifications:
* Bachelor's or Master’s in Computer Science, Statistics, Data Science, or related field.
* 1–3 years of experience in data analytics or data science roles.
